3 – About the use of Cookies

We use cookies on our website and apps to improve your browsing experience on our website. A cookie is a small text file that a website stores on your computer or mobile device when you visit the website.

Cookies allow us to recognize your browser and provide you with the best experience when browsing our website. In addition, cookies help us understand which sections of the website are most interesting to you and which content can be recommended to you.

You can block cookies in your browser at any time, or clear the cache to remove them.

It is important to note that we try to minimize the use of non-essential cookies, but we use the necessary ones to deliver a better service, as provided for in the legitimate interest of the LGPD. You can deactivate them in your browser at any time.

5- About your Rights in relation to the Data collected

With regard to personal data, the person has the right to access, correction, elimination, portability, limitation of treatment and opposition.

The person also has the right to revoke at any time that their personal data are used for marketing purposes and the receipt of commercial communications.

To exercise your rights, simply contact our team.
